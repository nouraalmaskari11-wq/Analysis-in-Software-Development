. Weighting and Estimation
4.1 Story Point Estimation

Story points measure:

Complexity

Effort

Risk

Common scales:

Fibonacci (1,2,3,5,8,13…)

Modified Fibonacci

Powers of 2

4.2 Estimation Methods
1. Planning Poker

Team discusses story

Each member privately chooses a value

Cards revealed simultaneously

Team discusses differences until agreement is reached

2. T-Shirt Sizing

XS, S, M, L, XL

Converted later into points

3. Bucket System

Quickly categorizes stories into “buckets” of size/complexity.

4.3 Weighting Stories by Complexity, Priority, and Value

Criteria may include:

Technical difficulty

Dependencies

Business value

Customer impact

Time sensitivity

The Product Owner uses these to reorder the backlog.
