Comprehensive Hospital Management System (HMS) Example

A Hospital Management System enables patients, doctors, nurses, and administrators to manage appointments, patient records, billing, pharmacy operations, and more.

This example includes:

✔️ 15 full user stories
✔️ Acceptance criteria for each
✔️ Complete, weighted product backlog
✔️ Covers: appointments, EHR, billing, pharmacy, admissions, discharge, lab tests, staff management, etc.

1. User Stories (15 Total)

Stories grouped by feature domain.

A. Patient Registration & Identity Management
US-01: Patient Registration

As a new patient
I want to register with my personal and contact details
So that I can access hospital services.
Story Points: 3

Acceptance Criteria

 Patient can enter name, DOB, ID number, address, insurance info

 Duplicate patient detection must alert staff

 System generates a unique patient ID

 Patient receives confirmation SMS/email

US-02: Patient Login & Profile

As a patient
I want to log into my online portal
So that I can access my medical information
Story Points: 3

Acceptance Criteria

 Login requires email/phone + password

 Patients can view/update contact info

 Password reset functionality works

 Account locked after X failed attempts

B. Appointments & Scheduling
US-03: Book Doctor Appointment

As a patient
I want to schedule an appointment with a doctor
So that I can receive medical consultation
Story Points: 5

Acceptance Criteria

 Patient selects doctor, date, and time

 Only available time slots shown

 Confirmation sent by SMS/email

 Appointment appears in patient dashboard

US-04: Doctor Appointment Calendar

As a doctor
I want to view my daily/weekly schedule
So that I can manage my appointments
Story Points: 3

Acceptance Criteria

 Displays upcoming appointments with patient info

 Allows doctors to block unavailable hours

 Real-time updates for cancellations or rescheduling

C. Electronic Health Records (EHR)
US-05: Add/Edit Patient Medical Record

As a doctor
I want to create or update patient medical records
So that I can maintain accurate health information
Story Points: 8

Acceptance Criteria

 Fields: symptoms, diagnosis, medications, allergies

 System logs doctor ID and timestamp

 Edits preserved through version history

 Records restricted to authorized staff only

US-06: View Medical History

As a patient
I want to view my medical history
So that I can understand past diagnoses and treatments
Story Points: 3

Acceptance Criteria

 Displays past visits, test results, prescriptions

 Records must be read-only

 Downloadable in PDF format

D. Billing & Payments
US-07: Generate Patient Bill

As an administrative staff
I want to generate a bill for a patient visit
So that the patient can make payment
Story Points: 5

Acceptance Criteria

 Includes consultation, lab tests, procedures, medication

 Insurance coverage automatically applied

 Bill downloadable and printable

 Payment status displayed (paid/pending)

US-08: Online Payment for Billing

As a patient
I want to pay my hospital bills online
So that I can settle payments easily
Story Points: 4

Acceptance Criteria

 Accepts card, mobile wallet, and bank transfer

 Receipt emailed after payment

 Payment reflected in billing history

 Failed transactions prompt retry

E. Pharmacy Management
US-09: Add/Update Medication Inventory

As a pharmacist
I want to manage medicine stock
So that we can track available inventory
Story Points: 8

Acceptance Criteria

 Add/update medication name, stock, expiry

 Low-stock alerts generated

 Only authorized staff can edit inventory

 Logs all inventory changes

US-10: Issue Medicines Against Prescription

As a pharmacist
I want to issue medicines based on doctor prescription
So that patient medication is handled accurately
Story Points: 5

Acceptance Criteria

 Pharmacist can view doctor’s digital prescriptions

 Issuing meds reduces inventory automatically

 Alerts for expired or out-of-stock items

 Patient receives itemized receipt

F. Laboratory & Diagnostics
US-11: Order Lab Test

As a doctor
I want to order lab tests for a patient
So that I can get diagnostic information
Story Points: 4

Acceptance Criteria

 Select test type and urgency level

 Notification sent to laboratory staff

 Test order linked to patient file

 Shows estimated completion time

US-12: Upload Lab Test Results

As a lab technician
I want to upload test results
So that doctors and patients can review them
Story Points: 5

Acceptance Criteria

 Upload PDF/Images or enter text results

 Auto-notify doctor and patient

 Results stored in EHR

 Results locked after submission (no edits)

G. Admissions & Discharge
US-13: Admit Patient to Ward

As a nurse
I want to admit a patient into an assigned ward
So that inpatient records are initiated
Story Points: 5

Acceptance Criteria

 Select room/bed based on availability

 Admission time and reason recorded

 Bed occupancy updated

 Admission visible in patient’s dashboard

US-14: Patient Discharge Summary

As a doctor
I want to create a discharge summary
So that the patient can leave with medical documentation
Story Points: 4

Acceptance Criteria

 Includes diagnosis, treatment, prescriptions, follow-up

 Summary auto-shared with patient portal

 Discharge time recorded

 Bed becomes available immediately

H. Notifications & Alerts
US-15: Hospital Notification Center

As a patient
I want to view all alerts and messages
So that I can stay updated about hospital activities
Story Points: 2

Acceptance Criteria

 Displays: appointments, bills, test results, reminders

 Mark as read/unread

 Sort by date and category
