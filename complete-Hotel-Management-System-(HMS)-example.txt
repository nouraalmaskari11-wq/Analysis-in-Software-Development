✅ Comprehensive Hotel Management System (HMS) Example

A Hotel Management System allows guests, staff, and administrators to manage reservations, check-in/out, rooms, payments, housekeeping, and more.

1. User Stories (15 Total)

Grouped by hotel functional areas.

A. Guest Management & Identity
US-01: Guest Registration

As a guest
I want to create an online profile
So that I can manage my bookings easily
Story Points: 3

Acceptance Criteria

 User can register with email, phone, and password

 Duplicate accounts rejected

 Email/phone verification required

 Confirmation notification sent

US-02: Guest Login

As a registered guest
I want to log into the hotel portal
So that I can access my reservations
Story Points: 2

Acceptance Criteria

 Login requires username/email + password

 Forgotten password flow works

 Account locked after repeated failures

 Browsers remain logged out after session timeout

B. Room Search & Reservations
US-03: Search Available Rooms

As a guest
I want to search rooms by date, occupancy, and type
So that I can find a suitable room
Story Points: 5

Acceptance Criteria

 Search filters: dates, number of guests, room type, price range

 Only available rooms displayed

 System updates availability in real-time

US-04: Make a Reservation

As a guest
I want to book a room
So that I can secure my stay
Story Points: 8

Acceptance Criteria

 Reservation includes dates, room type, price, number of guests

 System prevents double booking

 Reservation confirmation sent

 Guest can pay deposit online (optional)

US-05: Modify/Cancel Reservation

As a guest
I want to update or cancel my booking
So that my plans remain flexible
Story Points: 4

Acceptance Criteria

 Guest can change dates or room type if available

 Cancellation rules (refund windows) applied

 Updated confirmation/documentation sent

C. Check-In / Check-Out
US-06: Front Desk Check-In

As a receptionist
I want to check in arriving guests
So that they can begin their stay
Story Points: 5

Acceptance Criteria

 Verify identity and reservation

 Assign specific room number

 Update room status to "occupied"

 Print or send digital key card

US-07: Guest Check-Out

As a receptionist
I want to check out guests
So that their stay can end and room becomes free
Story Points: 3

Acceptance Criteria

 Final bill generated

 Room set to “needs cleaning”

 Deposit adjustments applied

 Receipt issued to guest

D. Billing & Payments
US-08: Generate Guest Bill

As a front-desk staff
I want to create an itemized bill
So that guests can pay accurately
Story Points: 5

Acceptance Criteria

 Includes room charges, taxes, room service, extras

 Auto-calculates total

 Can be exported or emailed

 Shows paid vs outstanding balance

US-09: Online Payment

As a guest
I want to pay my bill online
So that I can complete payments conveniently
Story Points: 4

Acceptance Criteria

 Supports card, digital wallet, and bank transfer

 Payment receipts emailed

 Partial payments allowed

 Payment history visible in guest portal

E. Housekeeping Management
US-10: Update Room Cleaning Status

As a housekeeping staff
I want to mark rooms as cleaned or in-progress
So that front desk knows room availability
Story Points: 3

Acceptance Criteria

 Status options: “clean”, “dirty”, “in-progress”

 Timestamp and staff name logged

 Updated status reflects in reservation dashboard

US-11: Housekeeping Task Assignment

As a housekeeping supervisor
I want to assign rooms to cleaners
So that workload is managed efficiently
Story Points: 5

Acceptance Criteria

 Assign task by room, time, and staff

 Cleaners receive notification

 Supervisor dashboard shows task progress

F. Room Inventory & Maintenance
US-12: Room Maintenance Requests

As a staff member
I want to create maintenance requests
So that issues are fixed promptly
Story Points: 4

Acceptance Criteria

 Request includes room, issue type, urgency

 Maintenance team receives notification

 Status tracked: open, in-progress, resolved

 Completion timestamp stored

US-13: Update Room Inventory

As an administrator
I want to manage room details
So that guests can book accurate room types
Story Points: 6

Acceptance Criteria

 Add/edit room type, price, capacity, photos

 Rooms can be marked active/inactive

 Updates reflected on booking portal instantly

G. Staff & Notifications
US-14: Staff Account Management

As an admin
I want to create and manage staff accounts
So that employees can access assigned modules
Story Points: 4

Acceptance Criteria

 Roles: admin, receptionist, housekeeping, maintenance

 Access control enforced

 Staff status: active/inactive

 Password resets supported

US-15: Notification Center

As a guest or staff member
I want to see all alerts and messages
So that I stay informed
Story Points: 2

Acceptance Criteria

 Notifications: booking confirmations, cleaning tasks, maintenance updates

 Mark as read/unread

 Filter by type and date
